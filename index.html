How it works

MVP Flow

1) Post / Apply

Employers create jobs. Students discover recommended gigs and apply with availability.

2) Confirm + Commitment

If accepted, student confirms and pays a commitment fee to secure the slot.

3) Complete + Get paid

Student submits work. Employer approves → salary released + fee refunded.

Why commitment fee?

Reduces no-shows

Students take jobs seriously, employers feel safer to hire.

Fair outcomes

Complete the job → fee refunded.
Fail to complete → fee forfeited (based on your platform rules).
